# Hero Image Generation Prompt

## Primary Prompt (for Midjourney / DALL-E)

```
A luxurious, modern plastic surgery clinic interior in Thessaloniki, Greece. 
Warm golden ambient lighting illuminates a pristine white marble reception area 
with subtle gold accents. A grand arched doorway with soft backlighting creates 
a sense of elegance and welcome. The architecture blends contemporary minimalism 
with classical Greek elements — clean lines, white walls, warm wood tones, and 
brushed gold fixtures. A single olive branch in a ceramic vase on a sleek 
reception desk. Soft bokeh effect. Cinematic composition, shot from a low angle 
emphasizing the grandeur of the space. Color palette: warm whites, soft golds, 
deep navy accents, natural stone textures. Ultra-realistic, architectural 
photography style, 8K resolution, shallow depth of field.
```
