## Page Specifications

### 1. HOME PAGE
- Hero section with CTA
- Doctor introduction snippet
- Featured procedures (6 cards)
- Before/After carousel
- Testimonials slider
- Trust signals (certifications, years of experience)
- Contact CTA

### 2. ABOUT PAGE
- Doctor biography
- Education & training
- Professional memberships
- Clinic tour / facilities
- Personal philosophy

### 3. PROCEDURES (Category + Individual)
- Category overview pages (Facial, Body, Non-Surgical)
- Individual procedure pages with:
  - Description
  - Ideal candidates
  - Procedure details
  - Recovery timeline
  - Before/After gallery
  - Related FAQs
  - CTA to book consultation

### 4. BEFORE & AFTER
- Filterable gallery
- Procedure categories
- High-quality image comparisons
- Patient consent notices

### 5. TESTIMONIALS
- Written testimonials
- Video testimonials (if available)
- Star ratings
- Verified patient badges

### 6. FAQ
- Accordion-style Q&A
- Categories: General, Before Surgery, After Surgery, Costs

### 7. BLOG
- Article cards with featured images
- Categories & tags
- Search functionality
- Related posts

### 8. CONTACT
- Contact form (name, email, phone, procedure interest, message)
- Google Maps embed
- Office hours
- Phone & email
- Social media links
- Virtual consultation booking

## Technical Requirements
- Bilingual: Greek (el) + English (en)
- Mobile-first responsive design
- WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility
- HIPAA-inspired privacy for contact forms
- Fast loading (optimized images, lazy loading)
- SEO-optimized (schema markup for medical business)
- Cookie consent banner
- SSL/HTTPS required
