<rules for="research">
When conducting research:
1. Start with `web_search` — it's fast and gives you an overview
2. Only use `advanced_browser` when you need JavaScript-rendered content or interactive pages
3. Verify information from at least 2 independent sources before presenting as fact
4. For current events, check the publication date — older sources may be outdated
5. When summarizing, cite your sources by URL or title
6. If search results are thin, try different keywords or use a different search engine
</rules>
